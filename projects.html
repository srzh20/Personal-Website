Reactive Sculpture

Inspired by the genre of ancient marble busts and sentient computers, the bust was designed to contrast the two ideas. Powered by and Arduino and 6 ultrasonic sensors, as viewers walk around the sculpture the RGB LEDs in the head of the sculpture change color based on were around the circumference the observer is and how close they are to the sculpture. The ultrasonic sensors allude to technology having many eyes. The face of the sculpture is also lit via a blue LED connected to one of the ultrasonic antennas

IPad Mini Case

The final design draws inspiration from Ellsworth Kelly and his use of color and shape. The stand and reading handle take inspiration from origami folding. The stand works in both the vertical and the horizontal positions. The back of the case is made of triangle forms which also make up the stand and the handle, which are fitted with hidden magnets so that they stay closed. The colors however span across the triangles, creating new shapes. The entire case is 3D printed aside from the addition of microfiber cloth on the back of the flaps to provide a more comfortable holding experience.
